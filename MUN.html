Know RNSIT-MUN SOCIETY

The RNSIT Model United Nations Society, established in 2023, serves as the RNSIT's premier platform dedicated to promoting critical thinking, public speaking, and intellectual engagement. In a short span of time, the Society has emerged as the fastest-growing student organization within RNSIT, gaining recognition for its vibrant community and impactful initiatives. Moreover, the Society has also formed collaborations with esteemed organizations such as KLE Law College MUNSoc, while societies like PES University EC MUNSoc and Dayananda Sagar University MUNSoc have acknowledged our growing presence in the MUN circuit.

OUR MISSION

Comprising a dedicated core committee of 16 members and a thriving community of 300+ enthusiasts, the Society is committed to its mission of cultivating confidence, enhancing public speaking abilities, developing global awareness, negotiation skills, eloquence and poise.

OUR EVENTS

NEXUS

In May 2024 and 2025, we hosted our flagship MUN event. Nexus 2024 was an Inaugural event comprising of a MUN Workshop followed by an Intra-MUN, bringing together young minds to deliberate upon an important global issue in a collaborative yet competitive environment. Nexus 2025 was the second successful edition held this year.

ATLAS

In October 2024 and 2025, we hosted Atlas, an inter-college World Affairs Quiz Competition that has attracted 250+ participants from over 10 colleges.

MEET OUR MEMBERS

The RNSIT MUN Society is led by a passionate team of 18 core members who ensure the smooth execution of all events, training sessions, and collaborations. From logistics to content and PR, each member contributes uniquely to strengthening our society and helping fellow students grow into confident global speakers and leaders.
